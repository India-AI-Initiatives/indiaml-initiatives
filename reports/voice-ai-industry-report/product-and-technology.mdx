## Product & Technology

### Core Platform Architecture

A vertically integrated platform that combines:
- **Telephony Engine:** SIP trunking, PSTN connectivity, and global number provisioning.
- **Media Engine:** Real-time media processing with WebRTC, optimized for low-latency audio streaming.
- **AI Engine:** Orchestration layer for STT, LLM, and TTS models, with tools for conversation design, and agentic workflows.

### Key Product Features

- **Low-Code Conversation Builder:** A visual interface for designing, testing, and deploying voice agents.
- **Agentic Tools:** Pre-built integrations for common tasks like CRM updates, appointment scheduling, and payment processing.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Real-time monitoring of call volume, containment rates, and agent performance.
- **Compliance & Security:** Features for call recording redaction, data masking, and adherence to regulations like GDPR and HIPAA.
